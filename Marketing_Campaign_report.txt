3. BUDGET RECOMMENDATION BASED ON ROI
- Insights: The Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) clearly reveals that the 'Final Campaign (Response)' and 'Campaign 4' generated the highest volume of customer conversions.
- Recommendation: To maximize Return on Investment (ROI), it is highly recommended to allocate 20% more budget to the marketing channels and 
  strategies that match the Final Campaign and Campaign 4 criteria. Conversely, budget should be scaled back from underperforming segments
  (such as Campaign 2) to prevent waste and optimize overall company profit.
